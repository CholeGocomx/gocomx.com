Target Customer Profiles · 目标客户画像
GoComX serves three distinct manufacturer and distributor segments seeking to access the U.S. retail supply chain · 三大核心客户群体
1
Emerging Brand Manufacturers
准品牌型工厂
Primary Segment · 最核心客群
Customer Profile · 客户画像
Established sales on Amazon and Walmart Marketplace with proven market traction · 已在主流电商平台建立销售基础
Pursuing expansion into major brick-and-mortar retail (Home Depot, Lowe's) · 积极拓展线下大型零售渠道
Possess structured SKU portfolios, UPC/barcode compliance, and retail-ready packaging · 具备完整 SKU 体系与合规包装能力
Representative Sectors · 典型行业
⚡ Power Tools & Hand Tools 💡 Lighting & Plumbing 🏠 Small Appliances · 小家电 🌿 Outdoor & Garden (BBQ)
Key Challenges · 核心挑战
Approved by major retailers, yet unable to complete EDI onboarding independently · 已获零售商认可，但受阻于 EDI 接入流程
Limited expertise in EDI transaction sets (850 PO, 856 ASN), label compliance, and routing requirements · 缺乏 EDI 报文、标签及路由合规能力
GoComX removes the final barrier between manufacturer and retail shelf — this segment demonstrates the highest willingness to pay. · 此类客户付费意愿最强，GoComX 直接打通其进入零售货架的最后一公里。
2
ODM / OEM Manufacturers
ODM / OEM 工厂
High-Value Latent Segment · 隐形大客户
Customer Profile · 客户画像
Existing contract manufacturers supplying major U.S. consumer brands · 现为美国知名品牌提供 OEM / ODM 代工服务
Seeking to launch proprietary brands through DTC or B2B retail channels · 计划自主建立品牌，拓展 DTC 或 B2B 零售渠道
Strong production capacity and competitive cost structure, but no established retail distribution · 具备强大产能与成本优势，尚未建立独立销售渠道
Key Challenges · 核心挑战
Unfamiliar with U.S. retail compliance standards and buyer requirements · 对美国零售合规体系及采购商要求缺乏系统了解
No EDI infrastructure — unable to connect directly with retail buyers without intermediary brand owners · 缺乏 EDI 能力，无法绕开中间品牌商直接对接零售渠道
GoComX provides a direct path to U.S. retail, enabling manufacturers to establish independent brand presence without relying on intermediary distributors. · GoComX 为工厂构建直连零售的通道，助力其摆脱对中间品牌商的依赖，实现独立品牌出海。
3
Cross-Border B2B Distributors
跨境 B2B 贸易商
Wholesale-Focused · 非铺货型
Customer Profile · 客户画像
Engaged in B2B wholesale distribution, not direct-to-consumer retail · 专注于 B2B 批发业务，非零售铺货模式
Operate overseas fulfillment centers with stable, recurring SKU portfolios · 拥有海外仓储体系，SKU 结构稳定
Key Challenges · 核心挑战
Retail and wholesale buyers mandate EDI compliance as a prerequisite for order placement · 采购方强制要求 EDI 合规，否则拒绝下单
Manual order processing creates significant operational overhead, error risk, and scalability constraints · 人工处理订单导致运营成本高、错误率高，严重制约业务规模扩张
Value Delivered by GoComX · 平台核心价值
Substantial cost reduction through end-to-end order processing automation · 全流程自动化，大幅降低运营成本
Expanded order intake capacity, enabling distributors to pursue larger and more diverse buyer relationships · 提升接单能力，助力拓展更大规模的采购合作
EDI compliance is the entry requirement for institutional retail. GoComX makes it accessible, affordable, and fast to deploy. · EDI 合规是进入规模化零售采购的门槛，GoComX 让这一能力触手可及。
